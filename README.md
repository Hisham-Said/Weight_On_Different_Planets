Planetary Weight Calculator:
Ever wondered how much you'd weigh on other planets? This Python program calculates your weight across the solar system! Enter your Earth weight, pick a planet, and see how gravity affects you. Shrink on Mars or skyrocket on Jupiter!
Features:
Calculate your weight on 7 different planets
Interactive menu-driven interface
Accurate gravitational conversion factors
Clean object-oriented design
Instant results with 2 decimal precision
Supported Planets:
Mercury - 37.6% of Earth gravity
Venus - 88.9% of Earth gravity
Mars - 37.8% of Earth gravity
Jupiter - 236.0% of Earth gravity
Saturn - 108.1% of Earth gravity
Uranus - 81.5% of Earth gravity
Neptune - 114.0% of Earth gravity
Run the program:
Enter your weight on Earth (in any unit - kg, lbs, etc.)
Choose a planet from the menu (1-7)
View your calculated weight on that planet!
Example
Enter your Weight on Earth : 70
In Which of the following Planet you want your Weight?
1.Mercury
2.Venus
3.Mars
4.Jupiter
5.Saturn
6.Uranus
7.Neptune
Enter your choice : 4
Weight Jupiter = 165.2
